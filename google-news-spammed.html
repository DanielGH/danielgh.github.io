Google News Spammed

As I do every morning (at 10AM CET), I started my day by opening up Google News. As I’m not a TV person but still like to know whats going on in the world, news aggregators are my goto source of international news, every day. Over the years, Google news has proven itself to be a good aggregator, showing me all the important past, current and upcoming newsworthy subjects.

On the Google News homepage, stories are split up in the following categories:

  • Top Stories
  • North Korea
  • World
  • U.S.
  • Business
  • Technology
  • Entertainment
  • Sports
  • Science
  • Health

I found it strange today that the Science section had only one article (from NASA) and while clicking the “More Science stories” nothing showed up. But that was not all, as the surprise got even bigger when I checked out the “Top Stories” sidebar. For anyone who is not familiar with the Google News Top Stories sidebar, its just a list of the most popular subjects of the day, ordered by popularity, the first being the most popular.

Today, the third most popular subject (after James Comey and Donald Trump) is “Roulette”. Yes, you’ve read it right, its Roulete. And when you click that term, a list of spammy “news” sites come up, all displaying the same type of gambling article related to Roulette.
